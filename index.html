As an American, I came to Japan, what did I get? I met the childish Wang Min. I met the elegant Liu Yanjun. I experienced the separation from my father. I got my AKB48 limited edition Levi's jeans. I have never regretted coming to Japan from the US and I think my decision to come to Japan from the US was the best decision I have ever made in my life. I never hate Liu Yanjun's Japan, I just hate Wang Min's Japan.
